# Third-Party Binary Notice: Neolink

The binaries in this directory (`neolink-linux-x64`, `neolink-linux-arm`,
`neolink-linux-arm64`, `neolink-macos-x64`, `neolink-win-x64.exe`) are
pre-compiled builds of **Neolink**, an RTSP bridge for Reolink IP cameras.
